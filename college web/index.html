CEC

The College of Engineering Chengannur (CEC), was established by the Government of Kerala under the aegis of the IHRD, in the year 1993. It has been approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and affiliated to APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU). Ever since its inception, the college has maintained its eminence as a leading engineering college in Kerala
